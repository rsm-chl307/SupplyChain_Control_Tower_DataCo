# Capacity Utilization

## Business Definition

Capacity Utilization measures the percentage of available manufacturing capacity currently consumed by demand.

## Formula

Capacity Utilization =

Weekly Demand / Weekly Capacity

## Business Purpose

- Identify potential bottlenecks
- Monitor plant workload
- Support production planning decisions
- Trigger proactive capacity management actions

## Alert Thresholds

Normal:
< 85%

Warning:
85% - 95%

Critical:
> 95%

## Stakeholders

- Supply Chain Planning Manager
- Manufacturing Operations Manager
- Business Process Analyst
