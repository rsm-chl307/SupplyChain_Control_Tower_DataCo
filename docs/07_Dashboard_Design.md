# Dashboard Design Specification

## Dashboard Objective

Provide end-to-end visibility into operational risk across production capacity, inventory availability, and customer fulfillment performance.

---

## Page 1 - Executive Overview

KPIs

* Average Capacity Utilization
* Critical Capacity Alerts
* Critical Inventory Alerts
* High Service Risk Events

Visuals

* KPI Cards
* Weekly Risk Trend
* Risk Distribution

---

## Page 2 - Capacity Monitoring

KPIs

* Utilization %
* Available Capacity
* Capacity Status

Visuals

* Utilization Trend
* Capacity Alert Trend
* Plant Comparison

---

## Page 3 - Inventory Monitoring

KPIs

* DOI
* Inventory Status
* Reorder Risk

Visuals

* Inventory Status Distribution
* DOI Trend
* Inventory Alert Trend

---

## Page 4 - Service Risk Monitoring

KPIs

* Service Risk Level
* Service Alerts
* Risk Drivers

Visuals

* Service Risk Distribution
* Risk Driver Breakdown
* Weekly Alert Trend

---

## Page 5 - Product Root Cause Analysis

KPIs

* Top Demand Drivers
* Demand Growth Drivers
* Demand Contribution

Visuals

* Top Product Demand
* Demand Contribution Pareto
* Product Growth Analysis
